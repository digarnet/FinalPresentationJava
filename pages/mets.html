Mets Advertising

The New York Mets took a bold marketing strategy this season by having a Super Bowl commercial to advertise their home game tickets. The ad only aired in the New York City metro area but was a bold statement that the team was going to be spending at the top of the market in all aspects of running a sports franchise. This bold new vision and willingness to spend big on ads is thanks to their new owner. Steve Cohen purchased the mets in November 2020 and is currently the richest owner in Major League Baseball. A bold hedge fund tycoon who is bringing an aggressive leadership style to the mets in many forms. One of those forms is advertising and using his resources the push the mets out front, not only in the New York City metro area but nationally.
